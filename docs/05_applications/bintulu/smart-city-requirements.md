## Use Cases

BDA has shown interest in **2 use cases** under the Smart Mobility initiative:

---

### UC1: AI Traffic Light

Adaptive traffic signal control using Edge AI.

**Required Features:**
1. **Real-time vehicle detection & classification** - detect car, motorcycle, truck, bus, etc.
2. **Adaptive signal timing** - adjust traffic light phases based on real-time traffic density to reduce congestion
3. **Multi-lane traffic flow monitoring** - monitor each lane of a junction separately for optimized phase control
4. **Incident & anomaly detection** - detect stalled vehicles, blockages
5. **Emergency vehicle priority routing** - priority signal for emergency or authorized vehicles
6. **Central platform integration** - connect to central traffic management system, scalable for city-wide deployment

---

### UC2: Smart Parking

AI-powered parking management leveraging **existing CCTV infrastructure** (Edge AI on existing cameras). Approximately **7,000 parking spaces** at a rate of **MYR 0.50/hour**.

**Required Features:**
1. **Vehicle detection & bay occupancy monitoring** - AI-based detection of occupied/vacant parking bays
2. **Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR)** - license plate recognition for smoother entry/exit
3. **Safety monitoring** - detect loitering, intrusion, accidents in parking areas
4. **Violation detection** - identify double parking, obstruction
5. **Real-time space guidance** - show available parking spaces to drivers
6. **Centralized dashboard & analytics** - operations monitoring, planning, and reporting

**Key Cost-Saving Benefits (selling points):**
- Minimal new hardware - reuses existing CCTV cameras
- Lower manpower & enforcement costs through automation
- Reduced maintenance vs. ground sensors
- Higher revenue accuracy and improved parking utilization
